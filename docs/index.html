Clockwise

Your old wall clock,
made smart.

A local-first smart-clock brain for the Ajanta OLC-501. Automatic time, adaptive brightness, presence sensing, a phone app, and Home Assistant — no cloud.

10 45

⚡ Flash it from your browser

No PlatformIO, no drivers to wrestle. Plug the D1 Mini into your computer with a USB cable and click connect. Takes about a minute.

Your browser can't flash — use desktop Chrome or Edge. You need to allow the serial port to continue.
Works on desktop Chrome or Edge only (they support Web Serial). After flashing, connect to the Clockwise-Setup WiFi the clock creates to finish setup.

What it does

🕐

Always-right time

WiFi + NTP with an RTC backup through power cuts. Handles daylight saving itself.

🔆

Adaptive brightness

Manual, day/night schedule, or a Sun mode that ramps with real sunrise & sunset.

👋

Presence sensing

A 24 GHz mmWave radar detects presence and distance, and can dim when the room's empty.

📱

Phone app

An Android app auto-discovers the clock for full control and sensor tuning.

🏠

Home Assistant

MQTT auto-discovery builds the whole device, plus a custom clock-face card.

🔒

Local & secure

Runs entirely on the clock. PIN-protected API and authenticated OTA updates.

After flashing

  1. Connect to the Clockwise-Setup WiFi network the clock broadcasts.
    A setup page opens automatically.
  2. Pick your home WiFi, enter its password, set an optional PIN.
    Your timezone is detected in the browser.
  3. Install the app or add it to Home Assistant — and you're done.
